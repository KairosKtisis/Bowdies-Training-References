Build

  • 3 Rum-infused Cherries

  • 3 dash Angostura Bitters
Method

Sazerac
Small Rocks Glass  ·  Mixing Glass + Strain
CocktailWhiskeyRyeSpirit ForwardStirredBittersClassicNew Orleans
Build
Ingredients
  • 2 oz Sazerac Rye
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 5 dash Peychaud's Bitters
  • ¼ oz Absente
Method
  1. Add ice to Small Rocks Glass immediately — set aside
  2. In mixing glass: add sugar, Peychaud's, rye, and Absente
  3. Add ice and stir until cold
  4. Empty ice from Rocks Glass
  5. Strain mixing glass contents into Rocks Glass
Garnish: Lemon Peel
