Bowdie's Old Fashioned
Small Rocks Glass  ·  Muddle + Stir
Buffalo Trace Rum-infused Cherries Angostura Orange Slice
Build
Ingredients
  • 2 oz Buffalo Trace
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 3 Rum-infused Cherries
  • 1 Orange Slice
  • 3 dash Angostura Bitters
Method
  1. Muddle orange slice, sugar, cherries, and bitters in Small Rocks Glass
  2. Add 2 oz Buffalo Trace
  3. Add Large Cube and stir
Garnish: Brûléed Orange

Whiskey
Sazerac
Small Rocks Glass  ·  Mixing Glass + Strain
Sazerac Rye Peychaud's Absente Sugar
Build
Ingredients
  • 2 oz Sazerac Rye
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 5 dash Peychaud's Bitters
  • ¼ oz Absente
Method
  1. Add ice to Small Rocks Glass immediately — set aside
  2. In mixing glass: add sugar, Peychaud's, rye, and Absente
  3. Add ice and stir until cold
  4. Empty ice from Rocks Glass
  5. Strain mixing glass contents into Rocks Glass
Garnish: Lemon Peel

Traveller Whiskey Honey Lavender Grapefruit Ginger Beer
Build
Ingredients
  • 2 oz Traveller Whiskey (or house bourbon)
  • ½ oz Honey Lavender Syrup
  • 1 Grapefruit, squeezed whole
  • 3 dash Black Walnut Bitters
  • — Ginger Beer (top)
Method
  1. Combine whiskey, honey lavender, grapefruit juice, and bitters in shaker
  2. Add ice and shake
  3. Pour into Water Glass
  4. Top with Ginger Beer
Garnish: Lemon Peel

Warner's Gin Lillet Pierre Ferrand Absinthe Rinse
Build
Ingredients
  • 1 oz Warner's Gin
  • 1 oz Lillet
  • 1 oz Pierre Ferrand Cognac
  • ½ oz Lemon Juice
  • ¼ oz Absinthe (rinse)
Method
  1. Combine gin, Lillet, cognac, and lemon in shaker with ice
  2. Shake well
  3. Rinse Martini Glass with ¼ oz Absinthe — discard excess
  4. Strain shaker into rinsed Martini Glass

Build
Ingredients
  • 2 oz Reposado Tequila

  • ½ oz Vanilla Syrup
  • 1 Egg White
Method
  1. Add ice to Small Rocks Glass immediately — set aside
  2. In shaker: add tequila, lemon, vanilla syrup, and egg white
  3. Add coil and shake (dry shake recommended)
  4. Pour shaker contents into the iced Rocks Glass
